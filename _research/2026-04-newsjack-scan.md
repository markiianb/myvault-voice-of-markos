## LinkedIn discourse map summary

LinkedIn shifted to relevance-based ranking March 2026; specific defensible POV wins, generic AI listicles get penalised. Discourse on Markos's 8 pillars is fragmented:

- **Pragmatic AI** owned by Pragmatic Engineer / Bindu Reddy / Ethan Mollick (enterprise-coded). **No one is doing pragmatic AI for households.**
- **Open-Source AI** owned by LeCun / Clem Delangue / Soumith Chintala (model-weights / infra-coded). **No one connects open-source AI to household sovereignty.**
- **Democratising AI** owned by Steve Nouri (GenAI.Works 14M) (SMB-coded). **Nothing on individual / family democratisation.**
- **Big Tech Accountability** owned by Doctorow / Whittaker / Tristan Harris (policy-coded). **Few connect critique to what families actually do this Saturday.**
- **Privacy Not a Tradeoff** owned by Andy Yen (Proton) / Patricia Thaine / Daniel Solove (enterprise/academic). **Almost zero personal/household evidence base.**
- **Can't Blame the AI** owned by McKinsey / AI ethics academics (enterprise-only). **Nobody covers household-level accountability.**
- **Simplicity** owned by DHH / Jason Fried / Maggie Appleton (design-coded, rarely AI-coded). **Nobody connects simplicity to AI judgment quality.**
- **Zero Trust** owned by Forrester / Kindervag (originator) / SecurityWeek / TrustCloud (all enterprise). **Markos can own "Zero Trust for the home" — pure white space.**

**Markos's three strongest plays:** (1) Pragmatic AI for households, (2) Big Tech accountability for personal data, (3) Zero Trust translated to households. Combined, no other LinkedIn voice owns this triangle.
