# Identity

## Bio

**Markos Symeonides — CEO and Founder, MyVault.** AI-powered B2B2C personal knowledge vault; V1 private beta. Based in Cyprus (previously Dubai).

**25+ years in enterprise software** across retail, logistics, manufacturing, banking, services, utility, energy, defence, and government.

Grew up in his family's software business, founded in the attic of the family home. Ran the company for roughly its last decade. The family sold it four or five years ago.

> *"We owned 100% of the company when we sold it and it was profitable. And we never borrowed a penny."* -- Markos, 2026-04-10

Three credential markers cleared for public use: fully held, profitable, never took on debt. Dollar amounts, buyer name, and personal proceeds are not cleared.

His team rolled out **Eloqua** (now Oracle Marketing Cloud) first in Europe, third in the world -- the go-to credential for go-to-market experience beyond technical delivery.

Has worked on sensitive government and defence engagements under **Official Secrets Act** constraints. Discusses only at the level of principle -- no agency names, no client names, no projects.

Hands-on with Bedrock, Claude, Qwen, Deep Seek, and smaller on-device models. Designed the hierarchical knowledge graph underpinning MyVault, among other systems.

- **Category:** **B2B2C personal knowledge vault.** Never "document vault." Never "document management." MyVault handles documents — but it's a knowledge vault, not a paperwork tool.

- **Hashtag:** `#KnowledgeVault` when category-tagging Markos's MyVault posts. Never `#DocumentVault`.
- **Terminology:** "Zero Trust encryption" — never "zero-knowledge encryption." See pillar 8 below and [[Decisions/ADR-0001-Zero-Trust-not-Zero-Knowledge]].
- **Signature phrase:** "knowledge graph" — the architectural detail Markos owns publicly.
- **AI framing:** AI is one component of a larger system, never the product itself (see pillar 1 below).
- **Challenger framing:** Challenge AI-industry narratives respectfully; never lecture, never preach.

These overlap with `voice/voice.md` rules. When in conflict, the brand-system positioning wins for product mentions; voice-of-markos rules win for personal voice.
