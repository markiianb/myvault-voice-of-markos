## How to read this list

**Confidence levels** (set by the research agents):
- **HIGH** = LinkedIn URL appeared in ≥2 public sources, with 2025/2026 posting evidence found.
- **MEDIUM** = URL confirmed via 1 source or recent activity hard to verify. Flagged for manual check.
- **LOW** = Plausible but weak — included only if unusually high-value. Manual verify required.

**The 50 are organised into seven mix-bands.** No band is sized to a hard quota — the goal is a balanced engagement surface that reaches: (a) AI/AI-product audiences, (b) privacy-and-trust audiences, (c) fintech/consumer/SaaS-CEO audiences, (d) mega-creator audiences, (e) operator/builder mid-tier audiences, (f) tech-journalism audiences, and (g) UK/family/Sarah-adjacent audiences that ground the brand in MyVault's primary ICP.

**Before engaging:** Sead should sanity-check each URL on first contact (click through, confirm right person, scan last-30-days posts). A short list of profiles flagged for manual verify is at the end.

**One caveat upfront:** the brief required ≥30/50 to have posted in the last 30 days. Agent research confirmed 2025/2026 activity for almost all 50, with most evidence dating from the last 60 days. The strict "last 30 days" check should run in a 30-minute pass before W1 engagement; profiles that fail it move to the bench in this doc's appendix and get replaced from the bench list below.
